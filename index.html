About Baragaon Nandur
Baragaon Nandur is a Village in Rahuri Taluka in Ahmednagar District of Maharashtra State, India.
It belongs to Khandesh and Northern Maharashtra region .
It belongs to Nashik Division .
It is located 36 KM towards North from District head quarters Ahmednagar.
15 KM from Rahuri Khurd. 215 KM from State capital Mumbai.
Baragaon Nandur Pin code is 413705 and postal head office is Rahuri Town .
